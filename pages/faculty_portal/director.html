Shri Pradeep Kumar Sarangi

Regional Director, Balangir

Professional Skills:

  • Leadership and team spirit
  • Diligent working spirits
  • Marketing and Administrative experience

Planning Skills:

  • Ability to undertake strategic planning
  • Self starter with good analytical skills

Pradeep Kumar Sarangi, MBA(Marketing), FMS, Delhi University, Delhi is the Director of Centurion University & JITM College, Bolangir Campus, Odisha. In this role and as a member of the leadership team at the University, he is responsible for the achievement of Centurion University strategic objectives through the dynamic role, direction and execution of a full range of effective and efficient Human Resources & Organizational Development practices and programs. His forsight leads Centurion University's efforts to continue to be recognized as a top employer, providing a progressive, diverse and inclusive workplace that is committed to both employees and students success and responsible for the executive leadership of the centre, including the academic, budgetary, and planning processes and strategic vision.
