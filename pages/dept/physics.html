Department of Physics

Course Overview

B.Sc in Physics:

As a department of excellence in imparting skill-integrated Physics education, it promotes high quality applications in Physics, nurtures research, and encourages higher and deeper learning among students and faculty members. The department also provides applications-oriented foundation level Physics courses to other programmes of the university. This is a 3-year programme and its curriculam are patterned after the CBCS B.Sc. Physics Honours system recommended by the UGC. In line with the focus on skills at CUTM, the department offers integrated elective skill courses like Computational Physics Skills, Nanotechnology Skills, and DIY Skills.

M.Sc in Physics:

This is a 2-year programme for students who have B.Sc. degree in Physics with Honours. Keeping in perspective the current research trends in Physics and employability prospects, the department also offers industry-oriented Material-science/ Nano-science courses.

Duration

B.Sc in Physics: 3 Years (6 semesters)

M.Sc in Physics: 2 Years (4 semesters)

Scope/Job Opportunity

After B.Sc in Physics:

  • Clinical Research Specialist.
  • Clinical or Laboratory Technician.
  • Laboratory Technician.
  • Plant Biochemist.
  • Environmental Consultant.

After M.Sc in Physics:

  • Research Specialist
  • Laboratory Technician
  • Science Advisor
  • Teacher & Lecturer

Eligibility Criteria

For B.Sc in Physics:

  • 10+2 (Science/ Arts/ Commerce) or equivalent examination with 45% marks and above.

For M.Sc in Physics:

  • BSc in respective disciplines (pass)
