2019

Traffic Awareness Rally

Centurion University Bolangir Campus and Centurion Public School Bolangir has entrusted Traffic Awareness drive on Dt.18/09/2019 to create awareness about the road safty rules among the students and common peoples of bolangir district.

World Pharmacist Day

World Pharmacists Day" has entrusted at CUTM Bolangir campus by the Students of School of Pharmacy to positive impact of health around globe with plantation of medicinal plants followed by awareness camp in nearby village Kharlikani. The event started in presence of Sri. Pradeep Sarangi (Director) of Bolangir campus.

FREE HEALTH CAMP BY THE STUDENTS OF PHARMACY CENTURION UNIVERSITY BOLANGIR CAMPUS

Health Care campaign was carried out in different areas of Bolangir town, villages by Students of School of Pharmacy,CUTM Bolangir campus. Health care campaign includes eye testing programme, Blood Pressure Test, Sugar Test, Haemoglobin Test etc. to provide health care to poor people where it is essential or needed.

SWACHHTA HI SEVA", on Gandhi And Shastri ji Jayanti

Swachhata Hi Seva, an initiative of the Swachh Bharat Mission. CUTM Bolangir Campus aims to encourage people to understand the importance of a clean environment. Leo Club Students of CUTM bolangir campus cleaned the campus and nearby surrounding with a positive passion.

2019 Fresher's Party

Centurions organised Fresher’s Party to welcome students of all the departments of first year. This function was organised by 2nd year students.The party started in the morning all the new students from every department in presence of all the faculty members, Principal sir and Director sir joined the party. Mr Fresher and Miss Fresher were announced at the end of the session.

CUTM EYE TESTING CAMP BY PHARMACY STUDENTS.

CUTM Bolangir Campus organised eye Testing Camp at Campus, bolangir toun and nearby villages. Camp provide free eye checkup to poor people where it is essential or needed.
