A Step To Fight COVIED-19:

Getting tested for COVIED-19 on Dt.-08th January 2021,The COVID-19 test of all theFaculties of our CENTURION UNIVERSITY JITM, CENTURION PUBLIC SCHOOL, CONCEPT BLOSSOMS SCHOOL, HELLO KIDS BOLANGIR has been successfully conducted and it's a pleasure to announce that everyone is found negative here.

National Youth Day Celebrations:

On the auspicious occasion of National Youth Day, i.e., the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda, our students enthusiastically participated and expressed their feelings. The Chief Speaker of the occasion Mr. Prasenjit Sinha, Lecturer in English, Govt. Women's College, Sambalpur, conveyed his ideas and enlightened the audience about the life and teachings of Swami Vivekananda. He too convinced the gathering about the present-day relevance of Swamiji's message and the ways to apply them in Dayto-Day life. He said, "Swamiji acted as the bridge between spirituality and materialism, as well as theories and practice." He too inspired the students and asked them to go through the writings and speeches of the cyclonicmonk.

National Cadet Corps, Senior Wing Boys & Girls for the Enrollment 2021 batch at Centurion campus:

It gives me immense pleasure to inform you all that Centurion University,Bolangir conducted the selection process for the National Cadet Corps, Senior Wing Boys & Girls for the Enrolment 2021 batch at Centurion campus. A Total of 37 cadets were selected by the Three Personal Instructor Staff from 11(O) BN NCC,Bolangir after conducting the physical test.
