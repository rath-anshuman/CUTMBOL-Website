PCNM PROGRAM (28TH Febuary 2020):

Centurion University Bolangir campus have successfully conducted National Seminaron Physics and Chemistry of Novel Materials (PCNM),to know more about the topic physic and cemistry.In the presence of Director Pradeep Sarangi sir the event have been successful completed and other members appricated the program ,SPY Tv member of centurion university have taken the byets of the dignetories , it was of two days programm and finished with cultural programm followed by dinner party and poster presentation. COVID 19 Awareness Programme in Kharlikani Village:

(13th April 2020) In the Leadership of Sri Pradeep Kumar Sarangi, Regional Director, Centurion University Bolangir Campus, an awareness programme was conducted at aware the villegers of kharlikani, Bolangir. Masks and soaps were distributed by the initiative of Centurion University Leo club members at kharlikani village,Balangir.

CENTURION PRIORITY TO SANITATION( 14th August 2020 ):

We all would have heard the quote "Cleanliness is next to godliness". The fact that cleanliness is a habit of keeping our body and surrounding clean. Cleanliness should be practiced regularly as a habit. The main mission is to encourage and make aware the younger youth towards the personal as well as environmental cleanliness. CUTM Bolangir Campus launched "Phenyl" as a part of it.

A Program On Fitness & Self Defence 14th September 2020:

Fitness itself might it be from stretching, flexing or aerobic exercise but at the end, it strengthens our body in all possible aspects.Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM) Bolangir Campus organised a program on fitness and self-defence for students on Zoom which has started from 23rd of September 2020. Good heath is crucial during this pandemic.

LEO Week 2020( 29th September 2020):

Conducting an awareness Bi-cycle ride camp to different places by Centurion University. Students of our CUTM Bolangir Campus ride bicycle all over the town to circulate awareness. Students and Staffs of every department had taken part in this Bi-Cycle Rally.

Cricket Compilation ( 15th December 2020):

Cricket is a game to enhance skill development and opportunities to develop personal and social skills. It is a team game which teaches us leadership quality, to be passionate, also makes us understand that hard work produces results. So to promote sportsmanship CUTM conducted cricket competition between students of Centurion University of Technology and Management, Bolangir Campus. Students of all schools has participated.
