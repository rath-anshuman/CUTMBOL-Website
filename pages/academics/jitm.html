About JITM

The JITM +2 Science was established in the year 2008 by Mr. Pradeep Kumar Sarangi with greater vision to impart ready to market based education in the remote area like Balangir under the mentorship of Prof. Muktikant Mishra and Prof. D.N Rao JITM +2 science has been imparting comprehensive knowledge in +2 Science subjects with excellence and guiding students of this periphery in choosing right career after +2, to enable them to be dashing , dynamics and competent to fit in needs of the presents rat race competitive millennium. Moreover it assures to develop self accomplished individuals. JITM +2 Science is running on its own greenery campus from the beginning of the educational year 2011-12. We have now spacious classrooms enriched with digital teaching- learning equipments, well equipped laboratories and boy’s & girl’s hostel under a single roof. JITM +2 Science has got permanent Recognition since 2013 and permanent affiliation since 2018.
